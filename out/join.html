For tutors

Please begin by filling the form below

Thank you for your interest in joining us as a tutor. To become one, you must first complete the application form below.

Application Form - Tutors (Google Form)

Once the form has been submitted, we will contact you within 48 hours.

Join our Reddit

You can then join our Reddit, by clicking on the link below. You will find all the necessary informations, as well as an opportunity to discuss with students and others tutors. Once your application has been approved, a Reddit moderator will assign the correct permissions to your account.

Joinour Reddit

Do not hesitate to contact a moderator, if you have any question.

Find students (for tutoring), and answer their questions

Once you have completed the previous steps, you should be able to post a "listing" on Reddit for tutoring services in your subject (as a volunteer), in order to match with students that correspond to your level and schedule.

(Optional) Get a @pythagoras.site Google Account and access to Google Classroom Premium

If you wish to obtain @pythagoras.site Google account, as well as a free access to Google Classroom Premium, please contact us either through our email: contact@pythagoras.site, or on Reddit (by clicking on "Contact the moderators")
